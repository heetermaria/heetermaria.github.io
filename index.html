Maria Heeter

About:

Maria Heeter is a journalist and incoming summer intern at Bloomberg News. She is an economics major at Scripps College and a contributor for The Student Life, the student newspaper of the seven Claremont Colleges, where she formerly served as editor-in-chief.

Previously, she was a reporting intern at The Sacramento Bee covering business, breaking news and coronavirus. She began her career as a freelancer for InDepth New Hampshire, covering state politics and the 2020 presidential campaign.

After attending high school in New Hampshire, Heeter moved to Claremont, California to study economics at Scripps College. She plans to graduate in 2022 and is currently taking online class from her home in New Hampshire.

She reports on business, real estate and higher education. She enjoys reading about technology and the intersection of economics and everyday life.
