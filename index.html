Maria Heeter

About:

Maria Heeter is a journalist and student at Scripps College. She is editor-in-chief of The Student Life, the student newspaper of the seven Claremont Colleges.

Previously, she was a reporting intern at The Sacramento Bee covering business, breaking news and coronavirus. She began her career as a freelancer for InDepth New Hampshire, covering state politics and the 2020 presidential campaign.

After attending high school in New Hampshire, Heeter moved to Claremont, California to study economics at Scripps College. She plans to graduate in 2022 and is currently taking online class from Berkeley, California.
